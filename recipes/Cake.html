Cake

Description

Baking a cake involves several steps. Here's a basic guide to get you started

Ingredients

  • 2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup unsalted butter (at room temperature)
  • 1 cup milk
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients

  2. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.

  4. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.

  6. Gradually add the dry ingredient mixture to the butter mixture, alternating with the milk. Begin and end with the dry ingredients, mixing well after each addition.

  7. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared cake pans. Smooth the tops.

  8. Bake in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cakes comes out clean.

  9. Remove the cakes from the oven and let them cool in the pans for about 10 minutes. Then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  10. Once the cakes are cooled, you can frost and decorate them as desired.
